# Clip Strap Assembly

For the purposes of this assembly, please ignore any precieved lengths of the strap, it has been greatly reduced to allow for visualization of steps.

## Step 0: Prepare parts

Cut 2in Nylon strap to 1.5 meters.
Using a torch, singe the ends so they don't fray. 

## Step 1: Position end of Nylon Strap

![](./exploded%20views/Step%201%20-%20Position%20end%20A%20of%20nylon%20strap.png)

The strap should overlap itself 1-2 inches. 

## Step 2: Sew

![](./exploded%20views/Step%202%20-%20Sew.png)

Using a sewing machine and strong thread, sew a thick Zig-Zag line where the above image is marked, making sure to go forward all the way and then reverse all the way back.
